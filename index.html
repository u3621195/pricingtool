Van Westendorp PSM

Use four price perception questions to estimate acceptable price range and key intersections.

ⓘ
How this model works
Method overview
  • Collect four price thresholds from each respondent: Too Cheap, Cheap, Expensive, and Too Expensive.
  • Convert responses into cumulative perception curves.
  • Find intersections to derive PMC, PME, IPP, and OPP.
  • Use the output to frame acceptable price range and pricing tension in a client-ready way.

Gabor Granger module
DEMAND & REVENUE MODEL

Gabor Granger

Use tested price points and purchase intent to estimate demand and revenue.

ⓘ
How this model works
Method overview
  • Present a respondent with one or more price points.
  • Capture purchase intent or yes/no willingness to buy at each price.
  • Aggregate purchase likelihood by price to build a demand curve.
  • Multiply demand by price to derive a revenue index and identify an attractive price level.
